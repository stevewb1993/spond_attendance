# Attendance Update — December 2025

Here's the December attendance roundup (data through Dec 20, before the Christmas break).

**Swim** — Saturday swim continues to be our strongest session, averaging 21 per session, up from 17 in November and ahead of last December (18). Monday swim also up nicely to 14 (from 12 in Nov). Wednesday swim has dropped to just 6 though, down from 8 in November and notably down from 11 this time last year — worth keeping an eye on. Technique on Thursdays holding steady at 10, and well up year-over-year (was 7 last December).

**Bike** — Tuesday bike averaged 12, down from 14 in November. Thursday bike didn't run in December. Broadly in line with last year.

**Run** — Club Run (Green + Associates combined) averaging 11 on Wednesdays, steady vs November and up from 8 last December — good trend.

**S&C** — Friday steady at 7, pretty much unchanged all year. Monday S&C averaging 6, up from 5 in November.

Main thing to flag is the Wednesday swim decline — everything else is either stable or trending in the right direction.
